coordinates 2D

A simple example of the coordinate encoder in 2 dimensions.

The coordinate is on a 90x90 integer grid and has a locality radius of 15 units. It maintains position, velocity and acceleration. Velocity is limited to 5 units per timestep. When the point crosses the horizontal axis, its vertical acceleration is reversed; when it crosses the vertical axis, its horizontal acceleration is reversed.
